Japanese Curry

Description

This recipe teach you how to make Japanese curry at home. This is a basic recipe so you can arrange it the way you like once you mastered this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Meat you like
  • Curry mix
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cut vegetables and meat
  2. Cook vegetables and meat
  3. Add water and simmer it until cooked
  4. Add curry mix
