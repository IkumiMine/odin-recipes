Omurice

Description

Omurice is a Japanese dish which a ketchup-flavoured chicken rice covered with an egg/omelette. This dish is very popular among kids and adults.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Ketchup
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cut onions and meat
  2. Cook onions, meat and rice with seasoning
  3. cook an egg with a different pan
  4. wrap a chicken rice with an omelette
