Fried Rice (Japanese)

Description

Fried rice is another popular dish in Japan. There are tons of various fried rice recipes. With this recipe, you can make a basic fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Green Onions
  • Your choice of meat
  • Salt and pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Your choice of seasoning like msg, chicken stock powder..etc.

Steps

  1. Cut onions and meat
  2. Cook onions, meat, egg and rice with seasoning
